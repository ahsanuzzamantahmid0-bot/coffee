Our Story

At Caffé Aurelia, we believe that a café is more than just a place to enjoy a great cup of coffee — it’s a community. We founded this café not for profit, but to create a space where people can feel seen, valued, and part of something bigger.

We proudly hire individuals from diverse backgrounds, including those with disabilities and people who have faced homelessness, giving them meaningful work and a sense of belonging. Here, everyone is part of our family. Our mission is simple: to serve not just coffee, but care, respect, and opportunity.

At Caffé Aurelia, every cup tells a story of compassion, inclusion, and community.
